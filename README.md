### About Me
I am a second-year Automotive Engineering student at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca (UTCN). While I am still in the foundational stage of my academic journey, I am deeply committed to exploring the intersection of traditional engineering and future-facing technologies.

My focus extends beyond the classroom; I am a passionate observer of the automotive sector's global landscape, specifically:

- Economic Fluctuations: Analyzing market trends and industry shifts.

- Technological Innovation: Following the evolution of EV infrastructure and ADAS.

- R&D Advancements: Staying updated on the latest breakthroughs in vehicle command systems.

- Social Impact: Understanding how the automotive industry shapes and drives the Romanian economy and society.

- Mechanical & Mechatronic Innovation: Studying the core mechanical foundations and mechatronic systems that serve as the "backbone" of modern automotive engineering.
